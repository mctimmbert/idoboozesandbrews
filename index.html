About Us

Skye and Sarah here, the faces of "I Do" Boozes & Brews!

We partnered up and founded "I Do" B&B in the spring of 2022. After years of working in restaurants, bars and catering companies we decided it was time to hit the road and begin our private bartending business. Together, we have a combined total of over 20 years in the food and beverage industry and 10 years in restaurant management. Our experience ensures that we can provide your guests an amazing bar experience. We put our clients and their guests at the front of everything we do here. We pride ourselves on being knowledgeable and efficient mixologists, and thrive on utilizing our creativity to create a unique experience for your guests. Want a unique custom cocktail, too? We have you covered. There is much more that goes into planning your event than you may realize! We have the skills and experience to properly plan and execute your event. Tell us your vision and we will make it happen, leave the planning to us! We’ll be with you every step of the way.

From the first phone call until last call, Boozes & Brews is here for you!
